Travelogue: Kyoto

Overview

Kyoto serves as the cultural heart of Japan, preserving thousands of years of history within its temple-lined streets. The city offers a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern Japanese hospitality that captivates every visitor. Exploring this destination feels like stepping back in time to the era of emperors and geishas.

Visitor Tips

  • Purchase a one-day bus pass for unlimited travel within the city.
  • Visit popular shrines early in the morning to avoid the largest crowds.
  • Always carry a small bag for your trash, as public bins are rare.
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes, as you will cover several miles daily.
